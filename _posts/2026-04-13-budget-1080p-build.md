A budget build works best when it stays balanced.

## Pick the target first
If you want 1080p gaming, keep the build focused on that. Do not pay extra for parts that only look premium on a spec sheet.

## Spend where it matters
A solid GPU and a decent PSU usually matter more than a flashy board or case.

## Keep it quiet
Noise matters. A build that is fast but annoying is not a good value.

My favorite budget builds feel boring in the best way: stable, cool, and good enough for the games you actually play.
