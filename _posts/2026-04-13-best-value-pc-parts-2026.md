Value parts are the ones that improve the whole machine, not just the box they come in.

## What usually matters
- A GPU that matches the monitor
- A PSU that you do not need to think about
- Enough RAM to avoid silly slowdowns
- Storage that feels fast in normal use

## What I would avoid
Avoid paying for cosmetic extras before you have a real performance need.

## Simple rule
If two parts are close in price, choose the one that makes the build easier to live with.
